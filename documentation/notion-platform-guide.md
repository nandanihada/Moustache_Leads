# Moustache Leads — Platform Documentation

> This document covers the full platform: what it is, who uses it, and a complete walkthrough of every panel, tab, and feature. Written for non-technical readers.

---

# What is Moustache Leads?

Moustache Leads is a **performance marketing platform**. It connects two types of businesses:

- **Advertisers** — companies that have offers (apps, surveys, sign-ups, etc.) and want people to complete them
- **Publishers** — partners who promote those offers to their users and earn a commission for every completed offer

The platform also has an **Admin** who manages everything — approving accounts, monitoring fraud, managing offers, and keeping the system running.

Think of it like a marketplace: advertisers list their offers, publishers promote them, and the admin keeps everything in order.

---

# The Three Panels

The platform is split into three separate dashboards, each for a different type of user.

| Panel | Who Uses It | What They Do |
|---|---|---|
| Admin Panel | Admin / Subadmin | Full control — manage users, offers, fraud, analytics, integrations |
| Publisher Panel | Publishers (Partners) | Promote offers, manage placements, track earnings, redeem rewards |
| Advertiser Panel | Advertisers | Create campaigns, track conversions, view performance |

Each panel has its own login and its own set of features.

---

# User Roles

| Role | Description |
|---|---|
| Admin | Full access to everything on the platform |
| Subadmin | Admin with limited access — only sees the tabs the admin allows |
| Publisher | A partner who promotes offers and earns commissions |
| Advertiser | A company that creates offers and pays for conversions |
| End User | A regular person who sees the offerwall and completes offers to earn rewards |
